    • Ingredients:

    1 ½ cups cooked or canned chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
    ¼ cup tahini (sesame paste)
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
    1 garlic clove, minced
    ½ teaspoon ground cumin
    2–3 tablespoons water (adjust for desired consistency)
    Salt to taste
    Optional Garnishes:
    Paprika
    Extra olive oil
    Chopped parsley

    Steps:

    1. Blend the Ingredients:
    In a food processor, combine chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, and salt.
    Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.
    2. Adjust Consistency:
    Add water, one tablespoon at a time, and blend until the hummus reaches your desired consistency.
    3. Plate the Hummus:
    Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl and use the back of a spoon to create a swirl on the surface.
    4. Garnish:
    Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika or chopped parsley.
    5. Serve:
    Serve chilled or at room temperature with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.
