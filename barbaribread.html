    • Ingredients:

    3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon salt
    2 teaspoons sugar
    2 teaspoons instant yeast
    1 ¼ cups warm water
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    For the Topping (Roomal):
    1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon sugar
    ½ cup water
    Sesame seeds or nigella seeds (optional, for garnish)

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Dough:
    In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
    Gradually add warm water and olive oil, mixing until a dough forms.
    Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–1.5 hours, or until it doubles in size.
    2. Make the Topping (Roomal):
    In a small saucepan, combine 1 teaspoon flour, 1 teaspoon sugar, and ½ cup water.
    Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens into a paste. Set aside to cool.
    3. Shape the Dough:
    Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C) and place a baking stone or baking sheet inside to heat.
    Divide the dough into two equal parts. Roll each piece into an oval shape about ½-inch thick.
    Transfer the dough to a parchment-lined baking sheet.
    4. Add the Topping and Score:
    Brush the roomal paste generously over the surface of the dough.
    Using your fingers or the edge of a ruler, create lengthwise indentations across the dough.
    Sprinkle sesame seeds or nigella seeds on top, if desired.
    5. Bake:
    Transfer the dough (with the parchment) to the preheated stone or baking sheet.
    Bake for 15–18 minutes, or until golden brown and crisp.
    6. Serve:
    Allow to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy with cheese, dips, or as a side to any meal.
