    • Ingredients:

    2 cups cooked or canned fava beans (drained and rinsed)
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    2 garlic cloves, minced
    1 teaspoon ground cumin
    Juice of 1 lemon
    Salt to taste
    Optional Toppings:
    Chopped parsley
    Diced tomatoes
    Chopped onions
    Hard-boiled eggs
    Crumbled feta cheese
    Extra olive oil

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Beans:
    In a small saucepan, warm the fava beans over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
    Add a splash of water if needed to keep the beans from drying out.
    2. Season the Beans:
    Add minced garlic, cumin, lemon juice, and salt to the saucepan. Mix well.
    Using the back of a fork, mash some of the beans to create a slightly chunky consistency.
    3. Plate the Ful Medames:
    Transfer the beans to a serving dish.
    Drizzle with olive oil and add your desired toppings, such as chopped parsley, diced tomatoes, onions, or hard-boiled eggs.
    4. Serve:
    Serve warm with pita bread for dipping.
