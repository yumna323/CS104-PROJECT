    • Ingredients:

    For the Base:
    2 cups cooked or canned chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
    2–3 pita breads (cut into bite-sized pieces)
    3 tablespoons olive oil (for toasting pita)

    For the Yogurt Sauce:
    2 cups plain yogurt
    2 tablespoons tahini
    1 garlic clove, minced
    Juice of 1 lemon
    Salt to taste

    For Garnish:
    2 tablespoons pine nuts or slivered almonds
    2 tablespoons butter (for toasting nuts)
    Paprika
    Chopped parsley

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Pita Bread:
    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    Toss the pita pieces with olive oil and spread them on a baking sheet.
    Bake for 8–10 minutes, or until golden and crispy. Set aside.
    2. Warm the Chickpeas:
    Heat the chickpeas in a small pot with a little water and a pinch of salt. Drain and set aside.
    3. Make the Yogurt Sauce:
    In a bowl, whisk together yogurt, tahini, minced garlic, lemon juice, and salt until smooth.
    4. Assemble the Fatteh:
    In a serving dish, layer the toasted pita at the bottom.
    Add the warm chickpeas on top, followed by the yogurt sauce.
    5. Garnish:
    In a small pan, melt the butter and toast the pine nuts or almonds until golden.
    Sprinkle the toasted nuts, paprika, and chopped parsley over the yogurt layer.
    6. Serve:
    Serve immediately while the bread is still slightly crunchy.
