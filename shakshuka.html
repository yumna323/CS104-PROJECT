  • Dinner

    • Ingredients:

    For the Base:
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    1 onion, finely chopped
    1 red bell pepper, diced
    3 garlic cloves, minced
    1 teaspoon ground cumin
    1 teaspoon paprika
    ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
    5–6 ripe tomatoes, chopped (or 1 can of diced tomatoes)
    Salt and pepper to taste
    4–6 eggs
    Optional Garnishes:
    Chopped parsley
    Crumbled feta cheese
    Warm bread for serving

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Sauce:
    Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    Stir in the red bell pepper and garlic, cooking for another 2–3 minutes.
    Add cumin, paprika, and chili flakes, and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    2. Simmer the Tomatoes:
    Add the chopped tomatoes to the skillet and season with salt and pepper.
    Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thickened.
    3. Add the Eggs:
    Use a spoon to create small wells in the sauce and crack an egg into each well.
    Cover the skillet with a lid and cook for 5–8 minutes, or until the egg whites are set but the yolks are still runny.
    4. Garnish and Serve:
    Sprinkle with chopped parsley and crumbled feta cheese if desired.
    Serve hot with warm bread for dipping.
