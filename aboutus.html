The website Halal Hub was created by 4 Architecture first year students at New Jersey Institute of Technology.
The 4 girls wanted to create a space easy to navigate and find exactly what you're looking for. Halal Hub
aims to help Muslims and anyone with similar dietary restrictions to find easy and delicious recipes to cook
without much effort. Halal Hub provides a wide variety of dishes to choose from, with detailed steps and ingredients.
