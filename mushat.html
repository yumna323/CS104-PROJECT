    • Ingredients:

    2 medium eggplants, peeled and cut into cubes
    1 large onion, chopped
    2 garlic cloves, minced
    3 medium tomatoes, diced
    1 cup cooked chickpeas
    2 tablespoons olive oil
    ½ teaspoon ground cumin
    ½ teaspoon paprika
    Salt and pepper to taste
    Chopped parsley for garnish

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Eggplant:
    Sprinkle the eggplant cubes with salt and let them sit in a colander for 20 minutes to draw out moisture.
    Rinse and pat dry with a kitchen towel.
    2. Sauté the Vegetables:
    Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until softened.
    Add the eggplant and cook until it softens and starts to brown, about 10 minutes.
    3. Add Tomatoes and Spices:
    Stir in the diced tomatoes, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5–7 minutes until the tomatoes break down.
    4. Add Chickpeas:
    Stir in the cooked chickpeas and cook for another 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
    5. Garnish and Serve:
    Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with chopped parsley, and serve warm with bread or rice.
