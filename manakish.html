    • Ingredients:

    For the Dough:
    2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
    1 teaspoon sugar
    1 teaspoon salt
    1 tablespoon olive oil
    1 teaspoon instant yeast
    ¾ cup warm water
    For the Za'atar Topping:
    4 tablespoons za'atar spice mix
    3 tablespoons olive oil

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Dough:
    In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and instant yeast.
    Gradually add warm water and olive oil while mixing until a dough forms.
    Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 8–10 minutes until it is smooth and elastic.
    Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a clean towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
    2. Prepare the Topping:
    In a small bowl, mix za'atar with olive oil until it forms a spreadable paste.
    3. Shape the Dough:
    Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    Divide the dough into 4 equal portions. Roll each portion into a thin, round shape (about 8 inches in diameter).
    Place the rolled dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    4. Add the Topping:
    Spread a thin, even layer of the za'atar mixture over the surface of each dough circle.
    Leave a small border around the edges to allow for a nice crust.
    5. Bake:
    Bake in the preheated oven for 8–12 minutes, or until the edges are golden and the bread is cooked through.
    6. Serve:
    Serve warm and enjoy as a snack, breakfast, or alongside hummus, labneh, or fresh vegetables.
