    • Ingredients:

    2 cups full-fat plain yogurt
    ½ teaspoon salt
    Optional Garnishes:
    Olive oil
    Dried mint
    Sumac
    Za'atar
    Chopped fresh herbs

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Yogurt:
    In a bowl, mix the yogurt with salt until well combined.
    2. Strain the Yogurt:
    Line a fine-mesh strainer with cheesecloth or a clean kitchen towel.
    Place the strainer over a bowl and pour the salted yogurt into it.
    Gather the edges of the cloth and tie them into a bundle.
    3. Drain the Liquid:
    Place the yogurt in the refrigerator and allow it to drain for 12–24 hours, depending on your desired thickness.
    Check occasionally and discard the liquid (whey) that collects in the bowl.
    4. Transfer and Serve:
    Once the yogurt has thickened into labneh, transfer it to a serving dish.
    Drizzle with olive oil and garnish with your choice of herbs or spices.
    5. Enjoy:
    Serve with fresh bread, vegetables, or as part of a mezze platter.
