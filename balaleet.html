    • Ingredients:

    For the Vermicelli:
    200g vermicelli noodles
    2 tablespoons butter or ghee
    ¼ cup sugar (adjust to taste)
    ½ teaspoon ground cardamom
    A pinch of saffron threads soaked in 2 tablespoons warm water
    1 cup water

    For the Egg Topping:
    3 large eggs
    1 tablespoon butter or ghee
    A pinch of salt

    Steps:

    1. Prepare the Vermicelli:
    Heat the butter or ghee in a large pan over medium heat.
    Add the vermicelli noodles and toast them, stirring frequently, until they turn golden brown.
    Add 1 cup of water and let the noodles cook until softened and the water is absorbed.
    Stir in sugar, cardamom, and the saffron water. Mix well and cook for another 2–3 minutes until the sugar dissolves and flavors blend.
    2. Prepare the Egg Topping:
    In a separate pan, melt butter or ghee over medium heat.
    Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pour them into the pan.
    Cook the eggs into a thin omelet, flipping once, and then slice into strips or serve whole.
    3. Assemble the Balaleet:
    Transfer the sweet vermicelli to a serving dish and top with the prepared egg.
    4. Serve:
    Serve warm as a breakfast dish or dessert, optionally garnished with nuts or raisins.
