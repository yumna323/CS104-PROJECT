    Lemon Butter Fish

    Article published, Dec 2, 2024

    Ingredients

    Fish
    • 500 g hake or kingklip
    • 100 g butter.
    • 1 small lemon
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 teaspoon ground
    • green chillies
    • 1 teaspoon ground garlic
    • 1 teaspoon chopped parsley or dry
    • 3 tablespoon mayo
    • Portuguese spice to taste
    Lemon Butter Sauce
    • 60g melted butter
    • 2 tablespoon lemon juice
    • salt
    • garlic

    Instructions

    Chicken
    1. Melt butter and mix in spices and lemon juice when cool add parsley.
    2. Marinate fish in above and leave aside for an hour Heat frying pan and gently cook.
    3. Fish on both sides spooning sauce over till done. Serve fish with lemon butter sauce.
